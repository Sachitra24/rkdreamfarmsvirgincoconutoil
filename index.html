Our Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Driven by Purity

At RK Dream Farms, we believe the best coconut oil is the one closest to nature. Our coconuts grow on lush, chemical-free groves in Tamil Nadu — sun-ripened, hand-picked, and cold-pressed within hours of harvest.

We are a passionate family startup committed to bringing farm-fresh goodness straight to your doorstep. No middlemen, no preservatives, no compromises — only liquid gold the way nature intended.
